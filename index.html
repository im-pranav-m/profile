About me

During the pandemic, I found myself diving into electronics out of pure curiosity. What began with simple circuits quickly grew into a passion for embedded programming—watching hardware respond to code was fascinating. That journey led me to Python, where I learned to build tools and automate ideas, and eventually into web and game development as I explored more creative ways to build and share things. Along the way, I also discovered a love for visual storytelling. I started using Blender to create 3D animations and scenes, and got into editing and design—learning how to bring ideas to life not just through logic, but through visuals and motion. I’m always exploring, always building, and constantly picking up new skills. For me, learning isn’t a task—it’s something I genuinely enjoy.
